About Satnam Stores

Built like a neighborhood staple, run like a family business.

Satnam Stores serves Prem Nagar, Indore with everyday grocery essentials, quick service, and a familiar local shopping experience that families can rely on.

The store combines household basics, trusted brands, and convenience-led ordering so customers can stop by in person or place an order on WhatsApp when they need a quick refill.

Family Business

Satnam Stores is positioned as a family-run neighborhood business focused on convenience, familiar service, and easy access to the products local households buy most often.

From dairy and pantry refills to beverages, bakery items, and instant grocery picks, the goal is simple: keep daily shopping quick, dependable, and close to home.
