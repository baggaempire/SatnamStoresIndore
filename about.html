About Satnam Stores

Satnam Stores is a Tuteja family-run neighborhood business focused on convenience, familiar service, and easy access to the products local households buy most often.

From dairy and pantry refills to beverages, bakery items, and instant grocery picks, the goal is simple: keep daily shopping quick, dependable, and close to home.

What makes it different

  • Family-led service with a neighborhood-first approach
  • WhatsApp ordering for quick pickup or delivery coordination
  • Convenient location in Prem Nagar, Indore
